This Privacy Policy describes how Color Picker (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use the application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to work primarily on-device and to collect no personal data by default.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Account credentials
  • Contact information
  • Precise location data

The App does not require user accounts.

2. Camera and Photo Access

The App may request access to:

  • Your camera (for live color picking)
  • Your photo library (to extract colors from images you choose)

How this data is used

  • Color sampling happens entirely on your device.
  • Camera previews and selected photos are analyzed only to extract color values.
  • No images, camera frames, or photos are uploaded or transmitted.

You may revoke camera or photo permissions at any time from your device’s system settings.

3. On-Device Processing

All core functionality is performed locally on your device, including:

  • Color detection and sampling
  • Color value calculations (RGB / HEX)
  • Palette creation and editing

No raw image data leaves your device during these operations.

4. Network Usage

The App may make optional network requests to fetch non-personal supporting data, such as:

  • Color or palette names
  • Informational content
  • Reference metadata

Important notes

  • No personal images or camera frames are uploaded.
  • Network access is not required for core functionality.
  • If you prefer a fully offline experience, you may revoke network access at the system level.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Local storage

  • Saved colors and palettes are stored locally on your device.
  • This data remains on your device until you delete it.

Removal

  • Clearing app data or uninstalling the App removes all saved colors and palettes.
  • We do not retain backups of your data.

6. Analytics and Tracking

  • The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • The App does not track users across apps or websites.
  • If analytics are enabled, they are limited to anonymous, aggregated usage metrics and do not include personal data.

(If analytics are added later, this policy will be updated accordingly.)

7. Third-Party Services

The App may display or link to external content sources (e.g., informational links). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is suitable for general audiences.

9. Your Privacy Choices

You can:

  • Revoke camera or photo access via device settings
  • Disable network access at the system level
  • Delete saved colors and palettes at any time
  • Remove all data by uninstalling the App

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App or on the App Store listing with an updated “Last updated” date.
